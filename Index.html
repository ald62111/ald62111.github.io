Dear Chick-fil-A, I will never forgive you for taking away high school BOGO

BOGO, for those of you who don't know, was a deal Chick-fil-A used to have for high school and college students that allowed them to get TWO WHOLE MEALS for the price of one. You got 2 entrees, 2 fries, 2 drinks, you even got twice the amount of sauces you would normally get no questions asked. Who needs two drinks!!!? No one is the answer, but Chick-fil-A gives you two drinks because they know how to make you happy in ways you wouldn't even imagine.

But, in all seriousness, Chick fil a bogo was one of the best periods of my life. Meeting up with anywhere from two to 6 of my best friedns, eating absurd amounts of food for one sitting and doing anything from having gang fights to going ding dong ditching. I forged bonds and memories that will last a lifetime during BOGO, while also saving money on good quality food. I miss BOGO dearly and nothing will ever be the same. For that reason, Chick Fil A, I will never forgive you, and I kind of hate you. But I don't hate you all the way, just because you're so, so good. I'm still mad though, so remember that.
